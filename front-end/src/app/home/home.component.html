Planning a cheap trip isn't easy,

why

When travelling on a restricted budget, there's an enormous amount of planning involved.

You have to use a number of different applications or platforms to plan your route, find the proper transport and get in contact with possible hosts through one of the hospex websites.

These are all problems we have encountered ourselves, and we try to provide a solution to these with Coucher.
